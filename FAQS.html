FAQs Answers
Can I return/exchange my order? Once opened, these items are non-returnable or exchangeable. If your order has not been opened, you can contact us at info@spiceworld.com to return the order for a refund.
How long does delivery take? Delivery takes between 3 to 5 working days
Are these products ethical? Yes, these products are ethical. We are SALSA (Safe and Local Supplier Approved) as well as committed to not harming the environment. All oru products are vegan as well however some are manufactured in factories containing nuts.
How can I contact you in regards to my order? You can contact us on info@spiceworld.com
