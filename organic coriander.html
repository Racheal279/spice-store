Coriander

Organic Ground Coriander is a wonderfully fragrant spice which is most well known for its use in Indian cuisine. Whether you use it in curries, stews or soups it gives a strong, fragrant flavour and a powerful nutritional boost. Organic coriander is one of the most popular spices used everyday throughout the world. This is probably because organic ground coriander is high in a range of nutrients including Vitamin C, Calcium, Magnesium, Iron and Zinc. Source: Egypt
